The Philippines: 3 Tourist Spots for your eyes to see!

The Philippines is a hotspot for many good tourist locations. There are many beautiful and natural scenery acroos the archipelago.

Boracay is one of the Philippine's most famous beaches. It is known for its white sand, the ones that create Boracay's pristine beaches and its clear, blue waters.

The Mayon Volcano is a stunning sight to see for the ones who have seen it. It is located in Albay, Bicola and it is an active volcano and something unique about it is that there is a local legend created for it.
Luneta Park is a historical landmark found in the capital city of Manila. It was the place where Dr. Jose Rizal, our national hero, was executed by firing squad.
